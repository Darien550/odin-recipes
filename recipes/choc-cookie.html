Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Try these delectable style chocolate chip cookies and you will never go back to older recipes!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  4. Cool completely and enjoy!
