Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza recipe made with mozzarella cheese and the perfect amount of pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • .5 cup water
  • .5 can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • .5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • .5 teaspoon onion powder
  • .5 teaspoon sugar
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • .25 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package HORMEL pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F. Grease two 12-inch pizza pans.
  2. Top dough with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly, 18 to 20 minutes. Rotate pizza pans between the top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
