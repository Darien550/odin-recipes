Buffalo Wings

Description

Enjoy the perfect buffalo wings to go with almost any type of party!

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • .75 cup all-purpose flour
  • .5 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • .5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • 20 chicken wings
  • .5 cup melted butter
  • .5 cup hot pepper sauce

Steps

  1. Place flour, cayenne pepper, garlic poweder, and salt into a resealable plastic bag, and shake to mix. Add chicken wings, seal the bag, and toss until well coated with the flour mixture.
  2. Transfer wings to the prepared baking sheet and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes, until cooked, and enjoy!
